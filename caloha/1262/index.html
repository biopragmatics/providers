Term Renal tubule

Long convoluted tubules in the nephrons. They collect filtrate from blood passing through the kidney glomerulus and process this filtrate into urine. Each renal tubule consists of a bowman capsule, proximal kidney tubule, loop of Henle, distal kidney tubule and kidney collecting duct leading to the central cavity of the kidney that connects to the ureter.
