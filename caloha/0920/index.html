CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Seminiferous epithelium

The epithelium lining the seminiferous tubules composed of primary male germ cells (spermatogonia) and supporting sertoli cells. As spermatogenesis proceeds, the developing germ cells migrate toward the lumen.
