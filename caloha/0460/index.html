Term Hippocampus

Located in the medial temporal lobes, surrounded by the entorhinal, parahippocampal and perirhinal cortices, lies the hippocampus. The hippocampus belongs to the limbic system. The dorsal part is involved in episodic memory and spatial navigation, while the ventral hippocampus is implicated in emotion-related connections. The hippocampus is made up of four regions or subfields: CA1, CA2, CA3, and CA4 which use the initials of Cornu Ammonis, an earlier name of the hippocampus. The term hippocampus is often used synonymously with hippocampal formation.
