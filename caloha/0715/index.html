CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Oral epithelium

Epithelium lining the oral cavity. The oral cavity is lined by masticatory mucosa, lining mucosa and specialized mucosa. The masticatory mucosa and lining mucosa have a keratinized or parakeratinized stratified squamous epithelium. The specialized mucosa is restricted to the dorsal surface of the tongue, where it contains papillae and taste buds.
