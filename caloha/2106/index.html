CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Large intestine mucosa

The mucosa of the large intestine contains numerous straight tubular glands (crypts of Lieberkuehn) that extend through the full thickness of the mucosa. The glands consist of simple cuboidal epithelium, as does the intestinal surface from which they invaginate.
