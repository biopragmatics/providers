CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Oviduct ciliated cell

Luminal epithelium mainly consists of ciliated columnar epithelial cells intermixed with non-ciliated secreting cells. Ciliated cell from the columnar epithelium that is part of a oviduct. Ciliated cells contain hair-like motile protrusions on the apical side of the epithelial cells that help in the mucociliary transport of gametes.
