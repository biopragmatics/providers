CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Oviduct epithelium

Epithelium lining the oviduct. Simple columnar epithelium composed of two kinds of cells, ciliated and non-ciliated. They represent different functional states of a single cell type. The epithelial cells undergo cyclic changings during menstrual cycle.
