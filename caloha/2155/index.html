CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Blood vessel vascular endothelium

Single pavement layer of cells which line the luminal surface of the blood vessels and regulate the transport of macromolecules and blood components from interstitium to lumen; this function has been most intensively studied in the blood capillaries.
