CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Cerebellum molecular layer

The top layer of the cerebellar cortex. Contains the flattened dendritic trees of Purkinje cells, along with the huge array of parallel fibers penetrating the Purkinje cell dendritic trees at right angles. This outermost layer of the cerebellar cortex also contains two types of inhibitory interneurons, stellate cells, and basket cells. Both stellate and basket cells form gabaergic synapses onto Purkinje cell dendrites.
