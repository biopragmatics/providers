CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Large intestine epithelium

Epithelium lining the large intestine. The mucosal epithelium of the large intestine contains the same cell types as the small intestine (enterocytes or absorptive cells, goblet, paneth, enteroendocrine and M cells) but paneth cells are normally absent in the adult human.
