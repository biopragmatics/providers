Term Small intestine mucosa

The villi (finger-like and leaf-like projections of the mucosa) and intestinal glands (crypts of Lieberkuehn) along with the lamina propia and associated galt (gut-associated lymphoid tissue) and muscularis mucosa constitute the essential features of the small intestinal mucosa.
