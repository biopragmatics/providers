Term Lymph node germinal center cell

The germinal center or reaction center is the activated center of a lymphoid follicle in secondary lymphoid tissue where B-lymphocytes are stimulated by antigens and helper T cells (T-lymphocytes, helper-inducer) to generate memory cells. Here, the germinal center of a lymph node.
