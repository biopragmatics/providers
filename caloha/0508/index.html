Term Kidney outer medulla

The part of the renal medulla that is nearest to the cortex. Each pyramid of the kidney medulla is dived into an outer medulla (adjacent to the cortex) and an inner medulla. The outer medulla is subdivided into an inner stripe and outer stripe.
