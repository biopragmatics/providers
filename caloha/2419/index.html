CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Cumulus oocyte complex

The cumulus-oocyte complex in the antral follicle is formed by fully grown oocytes surrounded by compact layers of follicle cells. Oocyte growth and acquisition of developmental competence occurs continuously throughout follicular development and is initiated as soon as the follicle enters the pool of growing follicles and is almost completed upon the antral follicle.
