CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Superficial temporal artery

A major artery of the head that arises from the external carotid artery when it bifurcates into the superficial temporal artery and maxillary artery. Its pulse is palpable superior to the zygomatic arch, anterior and superior to the tragus.
