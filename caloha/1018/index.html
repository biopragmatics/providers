CALIPHO Group Ontology of Human Anatomy
Term Telencephalon

Derived from telencephalon, cerebrum is composed of a right and a left hemisphere. Each contains an outer cerebral cortex and a subcortical basal ganglia. The cerebrum includes all parts within the skull except the medulla oblongata, the pons, and the cerebellum. Cerebral functions include sensorimotor, emotional, and intellectual activities. The anterior subdivision of the embryonic forebrain (prosencephalon) or the corresponding part of the adult forebrain that includes the cerebrum and associated structures.
