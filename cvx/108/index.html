Property rdfs:comment
This CVX is intended for use when one of the meningococcal vaccines containing serogroups A, C, W and Y (conjugate or polysaccaride) was given and the exact formulation was not recorded. It should not be used to record newly administered immunizations. It is not to be used when one of the meningococcal vaccines containing other serogroups was administered.
