GOBP_NEUROTROPHIN_SIGNALING_PATHWAY

A series of molecular signals initiated by the binding of a neurotrophin to a receptor on the surface of a target cell, and ending with regulation of a downstream cellular process, e.g. transcription. Neurotrophins are a family of secreted growth factors that induce the survival, development, and function of neurons. [GOC:bf, GOC:jc, GOC:signaling, PMID:17466268, Wikipedia:Neurotrophin]
