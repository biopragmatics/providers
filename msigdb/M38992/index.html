Methods: Liver progenitor cells were incubated in a WNT-enriched microenvironment for 72hrs (200 ng/ml mouse recombinant purified Wnt3A from R&D Systems). Independent culture experiments performed in triplicate include untreated cells or cells incubated with scrambled siRNA or with _-catenin-specific siRNA or with FZD8_CRD, alone or in combination with Wnt3A.
