GRYDER_PAX3FOXO1_ENHANCERS_KO_DOWN

We identified high-confidence PAX3-FOXO1 target genes by a series of criteria: first, using only PAX3-FOXO1 bound to enhancers recurrent in cell lines and tumors. Secondly, we only selected expressed genes, as PAX3-FOXO1 was not found in repressive chromatin. Third, we excluded nearby expressed genes if they were not found within the same topologically associated domain (TAD) as the PAX3-FOXO1 bound enhancer. Fourth, we also called out the maximally expressed gene within each TAD harboring PAX3-FOXO1. This approach removed 435 nearby but not expressed genes, and 78 expressed but out of TAD bounds. We found 1010 high-confidence targets, 678 of which were novel, and 439 were significantly reduced by shRNA knockdown of PAX3-FOXO1 for 48 hours. The reduced genes are represented in this gene set, which is a subset of GRYDER_PAX3FOXO1_ENHANCERS_IN_TADS.
