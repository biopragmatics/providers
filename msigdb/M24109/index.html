GOBP_PHOSPHOLIPASE_C_ACTIVATING_DOPAMINE_RECEPTOR_SIGNALING_PATHWAY

The series of molecular signals generated as a consequence of a dopamine receptor binding to its physiological ligand, where the pathway proceeds with activation of phospholipase C (PLC) and a subsequent release of inositol trisphosphate (IP3) and diacylglycerol (DAG).
