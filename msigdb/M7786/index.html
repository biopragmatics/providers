GSE23695_CD57_POS_VS_NEG_NK_CELL_DN

Thirty to 60% of CD56dimCD16bright NK cells in healthy adults express CD57, which is not expressed on immature CD56bright NK cells or fetal and newborn NK cells. We hypothesized that CD57+ NK cells within the CD56dim mature NK cell subset are highly mature and might be terminally differentiated. We used microarrays to assess the transcriptional differences between CD57+ and CD57neg NK cells within the CD56dim mature NK subset.
