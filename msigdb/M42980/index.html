GOBP_TIGHT_JUNCTION_ORGANIZATION

A process that is carried out at the cellular level which results in the assembly, arrangement of constituent parts, or disassembly of a tight junction. A tight junction seals cells together in an epithelium in a way that prevents even small molecules from leaking from one side of the sheet to the other. [GOC:krc, GOC:rl]
