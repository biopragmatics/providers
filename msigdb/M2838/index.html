A gene expression signature typifying the TGFbeta response in human epithelial cells was obtained from transcriptomic analysis of four human cell lines (Figure 1A, Supplementary Figure 1). These cell lines include HaCaT keratinocytes, HPL1 immortalized lung epithelial cells, MCF10A breast epithelial cells, and MDA-MB-231 breast carcinoma cells. The cells were treated with TGFB1 for 3 h in order to capture direct TGFbeta gene responses .
