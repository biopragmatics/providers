GOMF_VOLTAGE_GATED_POTASSIUM_CHANNEL_ACTIVITY_INVOLVED_IN_VENTRICULAR_CARDIAC_MUSCLE_CELL_ACTION_POTENTIAL_REPOLARIZATION

Enables the transmembrane transfer of a potassium ion by a voltage-gated channel through the plasma membrane of a ventricular cardiomyocyte contributing to the repolarization phase of an action potential. A voltage-gated channel is a channel whose open state is dependent on the voltage across the membrane in which it is embedded.
