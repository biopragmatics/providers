Molecular Signatures Database
PDGF_UP.V1_DN

To define ERK/PDGFR activation signature, SH-SY5Y cells were grown to confluence and starved overnight in serum-free medium in order to silence any sustained effects from growth factor signaling. Prior to induction with 50ng/ml PDGF, cells were treated with pathway inhibitors 74 mM Apigenin or 50 mM U0126, or with DMSO (carrier) for 60 minutes. Total RNA was isolated 30 minutes after PDGF addition. Experiments were performed in duplicate. The RNA was processed and hybridized with Affymetrix U133A GeneChips.
