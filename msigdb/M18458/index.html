GOMF_INTRACELLULAR_CALCIUM_ACTIVATED_CHLORIDE_CHANNEL_ACTIVITY

Enables the transmembrane transfer of chloride by a channel that opens in response to stimulus by a calcium ion or ions. Transport by a channel involves catalysis of facilitated diffusion of a solute (by an energy-independent process) involving passage through a transmembrane aqueous pore or channel, without evidence for a carrier-mediated mechanism.
