GOCC_PWP2P_CONTAINING_SUBCOMPLEX_OF_90S_PRERIBOSOME

A protein complex that forms a subcomplex of the 90S preribosome and can interact directly with the 5' External Transcribed Spacer (ETS) of the full length pre-rRNA transcript. In S. cerevisiae, it sediments at 25-30 S and is composed of Pwp2p, Dip2p, Utp21p, Utp13p, Utp18p, and Utp6p. [GOC:krc, PMID:15231838]
