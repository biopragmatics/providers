GOBP_FUSION_OF_SPERM_TO_EGG_PLASMA_MEMBRANE_INVOLVED_IN_SINGLE_FERTILIZATION

The binding and fusion of a sperm, with the plasma membrane of the oocyte as part of the process of single fertilization. In sperm with flagella, binding occurs at the posterior (post-acrosomal) region of the sperm head. [GOC:dph, GOC:jl, http://arbl.cvmbs.colostate.edu/hbooks/pathphys/reprod/fert/fert.html]
