GOCC_COPII_COATED_ER_TO_GOLGI_TRANSPORT_VESICLE

A vesicle with a coat formed of the COPII coat complex proteins. The COPII coat complex is formed by the Sec23p/Sec24p and the Sec13p/Sec31p heterodimers. COPII-associated vesicles transport proteins from the rough endoplasmic reticulum to the Golgi apparatus (anterograde transport). [PMID:11252894, PMID:17499046, PMID:22160157, PMID:8004676, Wikipedia:COPII]
