GOCC_ANCHORED_COMPONENT_OF_EXTERNAL_SIDE_OF_PLASMA_MEMBRANE

The component of the plasma membrane consisting of the gene products that are tethered to the external side of the membrane only by a covalently attached anchor, such as a lipid group embedded in the membrane. Gene products with peptide sequences that are embedded in the membrane are excluded from this grouping. [GOC:dos, GOC:mah]
