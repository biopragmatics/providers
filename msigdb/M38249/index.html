HP_ABNORMAL_TEMPER_TANTRUMS

A temper tantrum is an emotional outburst usually triggered by a sense of frustration and manifested as whining and crying, screaming, kicking, hitting, and breath holding. Temper tantrums are normal in toddlers and young children and usually happen between the ages of one to three years. Temper tantrums may be considered abnormal if they occur at an unusually high frequency, are of unusual severity, or occur at an old age than usual. []
