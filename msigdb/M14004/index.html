GOBP_INTERFERON_GAMMA_MEDIATED_SIGNALING_PATHWAY

A series of molecular signals initiated by the binding of interferon-gamma to a receptor on the surface of a cell, and ending with regulation of a downstream cellular process, e.g. transcription. Interferon gamma is the only member of the type II interferon found so far. [GOC:add, GOC:dph, GOC:signaling]
