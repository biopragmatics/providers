HP_EPILEPTIC_AURA

An epileptic aura is a purely subjective clinical manifestation of an epileptic seizure. If an epileptic aura is not followed by loss of awareness or propagation to a bilateral tonic-clonic seizure then it is a type of focal aware non-motor seizure. []
