GOBP_CELLULAR_RESPONSE_TO_ACID_CHEMICAL

Any process that results in a change in state or activity of a cell (in terms of movement, secretion, enzyme production, gene expression, etc.) as a result of a stimulus by the chemical structure of the anion portion of the dissociated acid (rather than the acid acting as a proton donor). The acid chemical may be in gaseous, liquid or solid form. [GOC:go_curators, GOC:mah, Wikipedia:Acid]
