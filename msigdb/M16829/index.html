GOBP_INTRACELLULAR_ESTROGEN_RECEPTOR_SIGNALING_PATHWAY

Any series of molecular signals generated as a consequence of an intracellular estrogen receptor binding to one of its physiological ligands. The pathway begins with receptor-ligand binding, and ends with regulation of a downstream cellular process (e.g. transcription). [GOC:mah, GOC:signaling]
