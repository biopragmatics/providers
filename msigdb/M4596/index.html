GSE24634_NAIVE_CD4_TCELL_VS_DAY10_IL4_CONV_TREG_UP

CD25+ regulatory T cells develop in the thymus (nTregs), but may also be generated in the periphery upon stimulation of naive CD4 T cells under appropriate conditions (iTregs). The mechanisms that regulate the generation of peripheral iTregs are largely unknown. We used microarrays to gain insights into the molecular program of extrathymic Treg development.
