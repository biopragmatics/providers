HP_CENTRAL_HYPOTHYROIDISM

A type of hypothyroidism due to an insufficient stimulation of an otherwise normal thyroid gland. Central hypothyroidism is caused by either pituitary (secondary hypothyroidism) or hypothalamic (tertiary hypothyroidism) defects. [DDD:spark, PMID:18415684]
