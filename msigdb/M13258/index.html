GOBP_FC_RECEPTOR_SIGNALING_PATHWAY

A series of molecular signals initiated by the binding of the Fc portion of an immunoglobulin to an Fc receptor on the surface of a signal-receiving cell, and ending with regulation of a downstream cellular process, e.g. transcription. The Fc portion of an immunoglobulin is its C-terminal constant region. [GOC:phg, Wikipedia:Fc_receptor]
