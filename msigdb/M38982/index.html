MEBARKI_HCC_PROGENITOR_WNT_UP

Methods: Liver progenitor cells were incubated in a WNT-enriched microenvironment for 72hrs (200 ng/ml mouse recombinant purified Wnt3A from R&D Systems). Gene pathways dependent on downstream _-catenin were studied by _-catenin knockdown with specific siRNA. Gene pathways blocked by extracellular SFRP-like Wnt inhibitors were studied by co-incubating cells with recombinant purified FZD8_CRD (300 ng/ml, from R&D Systems). Independent culture experiments performed in triplicate include untreated cells or cells incubated with scrambled siRNA or with _-catenin-specific siRNA or with FZD8_CRD, alone or in combination with Wnt3A.
