GOCC_SEROTONIN_ACTIVATED_CATION_SELECTIVE_CHANNEL_COMPLEX

A protein complex which is capable of serotonin-activated cation-selective channel activity. Mainly found in pre- and postsynaptic membranes of the brain and gastrointestinal tract. Depending on its location it transports Ca2+, Mg2+, Na+ or K+. It is always a pentamer, containing at least the 5HT3A subunit forming 5HT3A homopentamers or 5HT3A/B heteropentamers. In human, 5HT3A/C, A/D and A/E heteropentamers also exist. [GO_REF:0000088, GOC:bhm, GOC:TermGenie, PMID:16116092]
