GOCC_TIM23_MITOCHONDRIAL_IMPORT_INNER_MEMBRANE_TRANSLOCASE_COMPLEX

The protein transport machinery of the mitochondrial inner membrane that typically transports proteins that possess a matrix-targeting N-terminal presequence. The TIM23 complex contains three essential Tim proteins: Tim17 and Tim23 are thought to build a preprotein translocation channel while Tim44 interacts transiently with the matrix heat-shock protein Hsp70 to form an ATP-driven import motor. [EC:7.4.2.3, PMID:27554484, PMID:8851659]
