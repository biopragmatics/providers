GOBP_CELL_COMMUNICATION_INVOLVED_IN_CARDIAC_CONDUCTION

Any process that mediates interactions between a cell and its surroundings that contributes to the process of cardiac conduction. Encompasses interactions such as signaling or attachment between one cell and another cell, between a cell and an extracellular matrix, or between a cell and any other aspect of its environment.
