RFX7 is an emerging tumor suppressor that is activated in response to p53 and stress. Integrative analysis of the RFX7 DNA binding landscape (ChIP-seq) and the RFX7-regulated transcriptome (RNA-seq) in three distinct cell systems revealed direct RFX7 target genes. Direct RFX7 target genes identified in at least two of the three cell lines comprise a set of 57 genes.
